About Royal Challengers Bangalore

The Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) are a franchise cricket team based in Bangalore, Karnataka, India. They play in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008, the team is owned by United Spirits Ltd.

RCB is known for its star-studded lineups and exciting T20 cricket. They are one of the most popular teams in the IPL with a large fan base.

Team Information

Owner: United Spirits Ltd.

Foundation Year: 2008

Achievements

  • IPL Finals: 2009, 2011, 2016
  • Playoff Appearances: 2010, 2011, 2015, 2016, 2020, 2021, 2022
