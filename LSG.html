About Lucknow Super Giants

The Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) are a franchise cricket team based in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India. They play in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2021, the team is owned by the RPSG Group.

LSG is one of the newer franchises in the IPL, but have made a strong impact in their initial seasons. They are known for their dynamic team and exciting performances.

Team Information

Foundation Year: 2021
