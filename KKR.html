About Kolkata Knight Riders

The Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) are a franchise cricket team based in Kolkata, West Bengal, India. They play in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008, the team is owned by Bollywood actor Shah Rukh Khan, his wife Gauri Khan, and Juhi Chawla along with her husband Jay Mehta.

KKR has won the IPL championship twice, in 2012 and 2014. They are known for their exciting brand of cricket and passionate fan base.
