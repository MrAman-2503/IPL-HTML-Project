About Punjab Kings

The Punjab Kings (PBKS) are a franchise cricket team based in Mohali, Punjab, India. They play in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008 as Kings XI Punjab, the team is owned by a consortium of businessmen and celebrities including Mohit Burman, Ness Wadia, Preity Zinta and Karan Paul.

PBKS has reached the IPL final once, in 2014. The team is known for its aggressive batting style.
