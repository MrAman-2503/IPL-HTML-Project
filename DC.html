About Delhi Capitals

The Delhi Capitals (DC) are a franchise cricket team based in Delhi, India. They play in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008 as Delhi Daredevils, the team is owned by the GMR Group and JSW Sports.

DC has qualified for the playoffs multiple times and reached the IPL final in 2020. The team is known for its young talent and exciting performances.

Team Information

Foundation Year: 2008

Achievements

  • IPL Final: 2020
  • Playoff Appearances: 2008, 2009, 2012, 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022
