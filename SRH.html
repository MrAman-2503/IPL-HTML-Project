About Sunrisers Hyderabad

The Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) are a franchise cricket team based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. They play in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2012, the team is owned by Kalanithi Maran of the Sun TV Network.

SRH has won the IPL title once, in 2016. The team is known for its strong bowling attack and consistent performances.

Team Information

Owner: Kalanithi Maran (Sun TV Network)

Foundation Year: 2012

Achievements

  • IPL Champions: 2016
  • IPL Final: 2018
  • Playoff Appearances: 2013, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020
