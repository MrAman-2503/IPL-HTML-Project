Chennai Super Kings is an Indian professional T20 cricket franchise based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. The team competes in the Indian Premier League and was one of the eight franchises incorporated when the league was established in 2008

Captain: MS Dhoni

Owner: Chennai Super Kings Cricket Ltd

Founded: 2008

Coach: Stephen Fleming (Head coach)

Capacity: 38,200

CLT20 wins: 2 (2010, 2014)

Home ground: M. A. Chidambaram Stadium, Chennai
