About Chennai Super Kings

The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) are a franchise cricket team based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. They play in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008, the team is owned by Chennai Super Kings Cricket Ltd.

CSK is one of the most successful teams in IPL history, known for their consistent performance and strong fan base. They have been champions five times (in 2010, 2011, 2018, 2021 and 2023), and have qualified for the playoffs twelve times, more than any other team.

Achievements

  • IPL Champions: 2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023
  • Champions League T20 Winners: 2010, 2014
  • Most appearances in IPL playoffs: 12
  • Most appearances in IPL finals: 10
