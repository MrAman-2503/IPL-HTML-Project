About Rajasthan Royals

The Rajasthan Royals (RR) are a franchise cricket team based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, India. They play in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008, the team is owned by Manoj Badale (Emerging Media Ventures) and others.

RR was the inaugural IPL champion in 2008. The team is known for promoting young talent and its innovative approach to T20 cricket.
