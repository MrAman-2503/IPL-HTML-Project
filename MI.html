About Mumbai Indians

The Mumbai Indians (MI) are a franchise cricket team based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. They play in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008, the team is owned by IndiaWin Sports & Entertainment Ltd.

MI is one of the most successful teams in IPL history, known for its strong batting lineup and star-studded squad. They have been champions five times (in 2013, 2015, 2017, 2019 and 2020).

Team Information

Foundation Year: 2008

Achievements

  • IPL Champions: 2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2020
  • Champions League T20 Winners: 2011, 2013
  • Most IPL titles: 5
